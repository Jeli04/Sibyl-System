About Sibyl




Sibyl's Purpose


Sibyl is a Machine Learning powered chatbot supported by an informational and forum website. Our orignal goal for Sibyl was to create an interaction that felt humanlike to aid and talk to those who suffer from S.A.D., as if the bot was a friend.
